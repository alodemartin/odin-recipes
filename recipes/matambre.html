Rolled matambre

The rolled matambre is a traditional Argentine dish that consists of a stuffed beef roll. It is very popular at family gatherings and special events due to its delicious flavor and impressive presentation. Get ready to delight in this delicacy by following the steps in this recipe. Let's start now!

Ingredients

  • 1 1/2 kilo of beef slaughter
  • 4 eggs
  • 2 carrots
  • 1 bunch of parsley
  • 1 red pepper
  • 100 grams of grated cheese
  • 1 tablespoon of salt
  • 1 teaspoon pepper
  • 1 tablespoon oregano
  • 1 teaspoon ground chili
  • 1 handful of coarse salt
  • 1 vegetable broth cube

Steps

  1. To start with our recipe, we first prepare all the ingredients. We stretch the matambre to remove the fat. With a sharp blade, we remove all the fat from the matambre.
  2. Before starting to prepare, we cook 4 eggs for 10 minutes and set them aside. We roast the red pepper to peel it more easily. Chop the parsley and finely grate the carrot. Cut the red pepper into wide strips. We do all this to later fill the matambre.
  3. Now that we have all the ingredients ready, we begin to prepare our Argentine stuffed matambre. Season and sprinkle the matambre with oregano, ground chili, chopped parsley, carrot and grated cheese. To finish the filling, we distribute the red chili strips over it and place the hard-boiled eggs at the widest end of the matambre.
  4. We begin to roll the homemade matambre. Bending the two ends inward so that the eggs do not escape and, pressing firmly, we finish the procedure. We tie with a cotton thread to maintain the shape.
  5. Place plenty of water in a large pot with a handful of coarse salt and a vegetable broth cube. Once the broth starts to boil, we cook the rolled matambre stuffed with vegetables over medium heat for approximately 1 hour. When it has finished cooking, we turn off the heat and let it cool in the broth.
  6. Then, we remove it and press it for at least 5 or 6 hours. We put it in the refrigerator for at least 1 hour before consuming it. Enjoy it!

Ready! Now you can enjoy this exquisite rolled matambre accompanied by your loved ones.
